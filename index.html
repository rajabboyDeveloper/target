Our vision

“Our vision is to be the most trusted international school, where world-class education blends seamlessly with Uzbekistan’s rich culture. We prepare students not just with academic excellence, but with the practical skills, emotional resilience, and global awareness needed to thrive in tomorrow’s business world and top universities—all while nurturing their wellbeing and love for lifelong learning."

Our mission

At Target International School, we believe in nurturing not just bright minds but well-rounded individuals. Our world-class education goes beyond textbooks, fostering curiosity, creativity, and the kind of critical thinking that prepares students for life’s challenges. We’re committed to shaping confident, compassionate leaders by blending character education with essential 21st-century skills—because success isn’t just about what you know, but who you become. Every child’s journey is unique, which is why we tailor learning experiences to help each student discover and reach their fullest potential. And we know it takes a village— that’s why we work hand-in-hand with families, building trusting partnerships to ensure every student feels supported, inspired, and ready to thrive.
